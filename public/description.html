Moneyard is a mission-driven micro-investing platform and financial literacy hub that empowers underserved communities (especially in Africa and emerging markets) to build wealth through accessible, climate-positive investing and crypto education. We enable users to invest spare change into curated ESG portfolios, AI/tech stocks, and crypto while mastering personal finance via bite-sized learning.
